Welcome to HealthSphere

HealthSphere is a cutting-edge disease prediction system designed to assist users in identifying potential health issues based on symptoms. Leveraging a Naive Bayes machine learning model trained on extensive medical data, it provides accurate disease predictions and recommends the appropriate medical department for consultation. Whether you're experiencing unusual symptoms or seeking proactive health insights, HealthSphere is here to guide you with reliable, user-friendly technology.

Features

Symptom Analysis

Easily select your symptoms from a comprehensive list to receive a tailored disease prediction.

Machine Learning

Powered by a sophisticated Naive Bayes algorithm trained on real-world medical data for precision.

Department Recommendation

Get instant guidance on which medical specialist to consult based on your predicted condition.
